Core EDA Findings & Strategic Insights
High Impact Tenure (Correlation -0.376)

Average tenure for churned customers is 18.1 months compared to 39.4 months for retained customers. The risk is heaviest in the first 2 years.

Alert Support Calls (Correlation +0.179)

Churn rate starts at 8.57% for 0 calls, rises to 18.15% at 2 calls, and surges to 47.62% for 5 calls. Support interactions represent immediate friction.

Opportunity Contract Types

Month-to-month contracts exhibit a heavy 24.70% Churn Rate. Shifting these to 1-year contracts reduces churn rate to 8.26%, and 2-year contracts drop it to 1.92%.

Strategy Billing Sensitivities

Churned customers pay an average of $66.05/month vs $58.00/month for retained ones. Targeting premium billing accounts with loyalty deals is key.
